Porter Morgan

I'm a fifth year Math PhD student at the University of Massachussets Amherst, advised by Inanc Baykur. I study low-dimensional topology, specifically 4-manifolds. I'm interested in Lefschetz fibrations, nonorientable 4-manifolds, and exotic smooth structures.

Here are my research projects.

  • Irreducible 4-manifolds with order two fundamental group. Joint with Mihail Arabadji. Preprint in preparation.
  • Belted sum decompositions of fully augmented links. with Brian Random, Dean Spyropoulos, Rolland Trapp, and Cameron Ziegler. New York J. Math, 31 (2025):1-42, 2024.
  • Broken Lefschetz fibrations on nonorientable 4-manifolds. Thesis topic, in progress.

Here are a couple things I've been up to lately.

  • February 2025: Speaking at Louisiana State University's Geometry and Topology Seminar about my latest project on irreducible 4-manifolds with order two fundamental group.
  • January 2025: Attending the log cabin workshop about foliations on 3-manifolds in St George, Utah. Co-organizing a mini-course about the Thurston norm and fibered 3-manifolds for this workshop.
  • July 2024: Speaking at the Braids Reuinion Workshop hosted by ICERM in Providence, Rhode Island.
